# 🎓 VidVault: First 5 Minutes Tutorial

Welcome to the future of video management! In this quick tutorial, we'll walk through the essential steps to master VidVault.

## Step 1: Connect Your Library
1. Launch **VidVault**.
2. Click the **"Select Folder"** button.
3. Choose a folder with your video files.
4. Watch as your library instantly populates with thumbnails and folder-based albums!

## Step 2: Take Your First Note
1. Click any video to play it.
2. In the right-hand **Notes** panel, type something like:
   * `This is a key moment @ [[#0:15]]`
3. Click the **Preview** button (Eye icon).
4. Now, click the blue **[[#0:15]]** link you just made—see the video jump to that exact second?

## Step 3: Create a Smart Clip
1. Find a 10-second segment you love.
2. Type: `![[#0:00-0:10]]` (Replace with your actual times).
3. Switch to **Preview Mode**.
4. You now have a mini-video player embedded directly in your notes!

## Step 4: Organize with Tags
1. Add a hashtag to your notes, like `#ToStudy`.
2. Go back to the **Home** dashboard.
3. Look at your Sidebar—you'll see `#ToStudy` listed under tags.
4. Click it to find every video you've tagged, across all folders.

## Step 5: Save to Playlist
1. Click the **Three Dots (⋮)** icon on any video thumbnail.
2. Select **"Save to Playlist"**.
3. Create a playlist called "Best Moments".
4. Access it anytime from the Sidebar!

---

**Pro Tip:** You can link *between* videos! Try typing `[[OtherVideoName#2:00]]` to create a research web between different recordings.
